Hi, I’m Chrystal Gilbert, a web developer and UX-focused front-end designer who builds modern, responsive, and accessible websites for small businesses, startups, nonprofits, and personal brands. I specialize in creating clean, high-performance websites that combine strong visual design, user experience (UX), and front-end development. I have a background in Information Technology and hands-on experience working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Bootstrap, and WordPress (CampusPress). I use GitHub for version control and collaboration, and I design interfaces using Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign before translating them into fully functional, responsive websites. This allows me to move seamlessly from wireframes and prototypes to live production code.

As a front-end web developer, I focus on mobile-first design, responsive layouts, accessibility (WCAG), SEO best practices, and performance optimization. My goal is to make sure every website looks great, loads fast, and works smoothly across mobile devices, tablets, and desktop screens. Whether I’m building a portfolio website, small business website, landing page, or UX-driven web application, I design with real users in mind. I’m especially passionate about helping small businesses, entrepreneurs, and organizations establish a strong online presence through professional web design and development. A website should do more than just look good. It should build trust, guide users, and convert visitors into customers. That’s why I combine user-centered design, clear navigation, and clean front-end code to create websites that support business goals. If you’re looking for a web developer, front-end developer, or UX-focused web designer who can bring your ideas to life with both creative design and technical expertise, I’d love to work with you.
